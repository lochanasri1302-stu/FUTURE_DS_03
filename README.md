
# 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as **Task 3** of the **Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship**. The goal was to analyze marketing campaign performance and customer subscription behavior using **Power BI**. By exploring customer demographics, campaign details, and subscription outcomes, this dashboard provides meaningful insights that can help businesses make smarter marketing decisions and improve conversion rates.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze the performance of marketing campaigns.
* Understand customer subscription behavior.
* Identify factors that influence successful conversions.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for data exploration.
* Generate actionable insights to support better marketing strategies.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel (CSV Dataset)
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📂 Dataset

The analysis is based on the **Bank Marketing Dataset**, which includes customer and campaign information such as:

* Age
* Job
* Marital Status
* Education
* Contact Type
* Campaign Details
* Previous Campaign Outcome
* Housing Loan
* Personal Loan
* Customer Subscription (`y`)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* Total Customers
* Subscribed Customers
* Subscription Rate

### Visualizations

* Marketing Funnel Analysis
* Contact Method Performance
* Monthly Subscription Trends
* Job-wise Subscription Analysis
* Education Distribution
* Housing & Loan Analysis

### Interactive Filters

* Month
* Job
* Education
* Contact Type
* Marital Status

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Customer subscription rates vary across different demographic groups.
* Certain contact methods are more effective in driving subscriptions.
* Campaign performance changes across different months.
* Customer occupation and education level influence subscription decisions.
* Interactive filters make it easy to explore campaign performance from multiple perspectives.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Focus future campaigns on customer segments with higher conversion rates.
* Prioritize the most effective contact methods for better engagement.
* Schedule campaigns during periods with stronger customer response.
* Use customer demographics to create more personalized marketing strategies.
* Regularly monitor campaign performance to improve future marketing outcomes.
